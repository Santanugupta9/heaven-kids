About Us

We Change Lives,
Everyday

Established with a vision to provide world-class Montessori education in Nepal, Heaven Kids focuses on the holistic development of every child. We believe in learning by doing, exploring, and creating.

Holistic Learning

Mental & physical growth aligned.

Happy Environment

Joyful spaces for kids.

Advanced Child Lab

Modern STEM tools designed for little hands to explore science safely.

Dance & Music

Rhythmic activities that build coordination, confidence, and joy.

Monthly Doctor Checkup

Regular health screenings every 30 days by pediatric professionals.

Practical Learning

Emphasis on learning by doing through real-world examples.

Cultural Classes

Celebrating diversity, traditions, and festivals together.

Spacious Classrooms

Open, airy environments that encourage free movement.

CCTV Secured

Nutritious Meals

Green Environment

Customized Classrooms

Transportation

Day Care Facility

Sleep & Play Zones
